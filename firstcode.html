VIDEO: We were shaking when we found out – Davido confirms welcoming set of twins with wife, Chioma

15th October 2023

Multiple-award-winning artiste, David Adeleke, popularly known as Davido, has confirmed welcoming a set of twins with his wife, Chioma Rowland.

News surfaced on Friday that the ‘Unavailable’ crooner and his wife welcomed a set of twins, a boy and a girl, in the United States.

Confirming the news for the first time, Davido, while speaking at the United Masters Select Con event Saturday, said he and his wife were shaking upon finding out they were expecting twins.

The singer, who lost his son, Ifeanyi, in October 2022, speaking on his healing journey following the death of his son and the birth of his twins, said, “When my wife and I found out, we were shaking, and it was in the same month my son passed. My son passed away last year in October, and my wife gave birth this year in October.”
